Asset conversion lineage

Every accepted original format, its current compiler route into a native type, and the intended Bevy or renderer handoff. Source archives stop at preflight; the game opens only a validated .ozp pack.

.ozmodel, .oztexture, and the other native extensions below name typed virtual entries inside the installed .ozp; they are not separate installed pack files. The functional specification defines their owners, and behavior coverage measures the downstream logic closure.

Current proof, 2026-07-21: the selected three-core-archive corpus produced and installed one validated 4,998,541,776-byte openzt2.ozp. A mapped row below means that a source-to-pack-to-loader route exists. It does not mean every archived field has a consumer or that the original behavior is complete. Known downstream gaps include catalogue row hydration, several gameplay consumers, and the temporary texture residency bridge; Bink video remains blocked.

Staged compilerDiscovery, verification, precedence planning, translation, validation, assembly, and atomic installation are separate bounded phases with byte-accurate progress.
Mapped custom assetsAll immutable archives remain ranges inside one Arc<MappedPack>. Bevy handles point to custom asset wrappers over those ranges.
Bevy-native zero-copyNone of Bevy’s stock payload assets currently borrow arbitrary mmap ranges. Custom mapped preparation is used where implemented; stock Bevy assets own their normal payload storage.
Owned preparationBounded metadata, decoder state, ECS components, and GPU resources are expected. Current model loading also constructs owned Bevy Mesh assets; removing avoidable payload copies remains an acceptance item rather than a claimed result.

The Vec fields visible in converter-side Rust structures are archive-construction inputs. After rkyv serialization they are archived vectors traversed in place; they are not reconstructed as runtime heap vectors. Small handle arrays, draw metadata, pose buffers, decoder state, and GPU objects are deliberate runtime state rather than copies of source payloads.
